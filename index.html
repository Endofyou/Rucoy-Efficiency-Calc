Rucoy Training Calculator

Input into the following fields the corresponding information to receive training insights.

Overview:
This calculator will indicate the best monster for you to train, the average training duration until the monster dies (keep in mind that monsters exhaust after 7 minutes 30 seconds), and the stats needed to progress to the next monster.

"Enable Power Training" explained:
If this box is unchecked, the calculator will show results for afk training. Enabling the checkbox will indicate to the calculator that you are power training. If you are a mage wanting to power train, ensure to also select "Enable Magic".

Parameters explained:
"Base level" is the level that is seen above your character's head. "Skill Level" is the stat level that you intend to train. "Extra Equips" refers to the stat-boosting ring/neck/armor that you will be wearing while training, all added together (negative value for void equips). "Weapon Attack" is the strength of your training weapon, which can be found by reading its attack value from the inventory.

Advanced Options explained:
"Desired Efficiency" allows you to choose how efficient you want your training to be. For example, 90% efficiency will only show monsters that you can afk train at a rate of 3,240 ticks per hour or better, or power train at a rate of 12,960 ticks per hour or better; the max value is 99%. "Enable Crit Ring" indicates to the calculator that you intend to use a crit ring while training.

Limitations:
This calculator unfortunately cannot indicate whether or not you are able to tank the resultant monster. Therefore, it is possible that the calculator may reccomend a monster that is too strong for you.
